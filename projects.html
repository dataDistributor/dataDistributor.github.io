VPN Stability for Remote Staff

VPN Network Troubleshooting SaaS

Stabilized a remote workforce VPN deployment and dramatically reduced disconnect-related tickets.

View details

Problem: Remote employees experienced frequent VPN disconnects during work hours, impacting productivity and increasing ticket volume.

Action: Collected and analyzed client-side logs and server metrics, identified MTU and keepalive misconfiguration, then deployed policy changes and monitoring dashboards.

Result: VPN uptime improved to 99.8% and related support tickets dropped by 85% over the following month.
